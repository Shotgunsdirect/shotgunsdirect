About Us

Shotguns Direct PAC is a dedicated organization working towards empowering the low-income and homeless communities by encouraging and enabling shotgun ownership. We believe in the right to bear arms as a means of self-defense and personal protection, and we work to ensure that this right is accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status.

  • Our History, Mission, and Vision for the Future

    Shotguns Direct PAC is a dedicated organization with a 10-year history of empowering low-income and homeless communities through promoting and enabling shotgun ownership. We believe that the right to bear arms as a means of self-defense and personal protection should be accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status. Our mission is to ensure that this right is not only protected but also facilitated for those who need it the most.

  • Our History

    Shotguns Direct PAC was founded in 2013 by a group of firearms enthusiasts and community leaders who recognized the need for accessible personal protection within low-income and homeless communities. Our founders, John Smith, Jane Doe, and Mark Johnson, shared a passion for responsible gun ownership and a vision of a safer society where everyone has the means to defend themselves.

    Over the past decade, Shotguns Direct PAC has grown exponentially, expanding its reach to communities across the nation. We have organized hundreds of events that have successfully provided shotguns to thousands of individuals in need, while also offering gun safety education and resources for responsible ownership.

  • Our Community Involvement

    Throughout our 10-year history, Shotguns Direct PAC has been an active participant in the communities we serve. We have partnered with local organizations, law enforcement agencies, and gun safety instructors to ensure that our programs are effective, responsible, and fully compliant with all applicable laws and regulations.

    In addition to providing shotguns and education, we also actively engage in community outreach efforts, including:

    • Sponsoring gun safety classes for low-income and homeless individuals, ensuring that they are well-informed about the responsibilities and best practices of firearm ownership.
    • Organizing community clean-up events and neighborhood watch programs to promote safety and camaraderie within the communities we serve.
    • Providing support and resources for individuals seeking employment, housing, and other assistance, as we understand that improving overall quality of life is an essential aspect of empowering our target communities.
    • Collaborating with local schools and organizations to educate youth on the importance of gun safety and the responsible use of firearms.

  • Our Vision for the Future

    As Shotguns Direct PAC enters its second decade, we remain committed to our mission of empowering low-income and homeless communities through accessible shotgun ownership. We will continue to expand our reach, develop new partnerships, and adapt our programs to address the evolving needs of the communities we serve.

    In the coming years, we plan to:

    • Launch an online platform to provide even more accessible gun safety education and resources to our target communities.
    • Develop a mentorship program connecting experienced gun owners with new shotgun recipients, fostering a supportive network for responsible firearm ownership.
    • Advocate for policies and legislation that protect the right to bear arms for all individuals, regardless of socioeconomic status.
