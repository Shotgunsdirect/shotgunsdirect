Support

Support Shotguns Direct PAC - Make a Difference Today

  1. Home
  2. Support

At Shotguns Direct PAC, we are dedicated to empowering low-income and homeless communities by promoting and enabling shotgun ownership. Your support is vital in helping us achieve our mission and make a lasting impact on the lives of those we serve. There are many ways to contribute and show your support, from making a monetary donation to volunteering your time and expertise.

Donations

Make a Donation: Your financial contribution helps us fund our events, provide shotguns to those in need, and continue our community outreach efforts. You can make a one-time donation or set up a recurring monthly contribution. Every dollar makes a difference!

Donate Now

Volunteering

Volunteer: We are always looking for dedicated volunteers to help with our events, community initiatives, and organizational tasks. Whether you have experience with firearms, event planning, or simply a passion for helping others, we would love to have you on our team!

Volunteer Signup

Attend

Attend Our Events: Your presence at our events not only shows your support for our cause but also helps spread awareness about the importance of accessible shotgun ownership for low-income and homeless communities. Join us at our next event and help make a positive impact!
