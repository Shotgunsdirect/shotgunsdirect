Welcome to Shotguns Direct!

Welcome to Shotguns Direct PAC, a dedicated organization working towards empowering the low-income and homeless communities by encouraging and enabling shotgun ownership. We believe in the right to bear arms as a means of self-defense and personal protection, and we work to ensure that this right is accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status.

Learn More

Events

Shotguns Direct PAC organizes events that showcase the benefits of shotgun ownership for our target communities. By working within the governmental systems and adhering to all applicable laws, we help provide shotguns to those in need. To learn more about our upcoming events and how you can participate, please visit our Events page.

Learn More

Waiting Periods

We follow all waiting period requirements as mandated by law. If a waiting period is required, event participants can choose to pick up their shotgun at our local office or at the next scheduled event. For more information on waiting periods and pickup options, please visit our FAQ page.

Get involved

Join us in our mission to empower low-income and homeless communities through shotgun ownership. Support Shotguns Direct PAC by making a donation, volunteering your time, or attending our events. Together, we can make a difference!

The Defender 500 - A Simple, Reliable, and Easy-to-Use Shotgun

Empowering Low-Income and Homeless Communities with Accessible Firearm Solutions

Introducing the Defender 500, a simple, reliable, and easy-to-use shotgun designed with accessibility and ease of use in mind. The Defender 500 is perfect for individuals seeking a straightforward firearm for personal protection and self-defense.

Key Features:

    Pump-Action Mechanism: The Defender 500 utilizes a dependable pump-action mechanism that allows for smooth and efficient operation. This design ensures reliable performance while minimizing the risk of jamming or misfires.
  • Lightweight and Compact: With its lightweight design and compact size, the Defender 500 is easy to handle and maneuver, making it suitable for individuals with varying levels of physical strength and firearm experience.
  • Durable Construction: Built with high-quality materials and a robust construction, the Defender 500 is designed to withstand the test of time, providing long-lasting reliability and performance.
  • Easy Maintenance: The Defender 500 is designed for simple maintenance, with easily accessible components and straightforward disassembly and reassembly procedures. This makes it an ideal option for those with limited experience in firearm maintenance.
  • Safety Features: The Defender 500 is equipped with essential safety features, such as a manual safety and a trigger guard, to help prevent accidental discharges and promote responsible firearm handling.

The Defender 500 is an excellent choice for those in low-income and homeless communities seeking a simple and reliable shotgun for personal protection. Its user-friendly design, ease of maintenance, and essential safety features make it an accessible option for individuals of all experience levels. Experience the peace of mind that comes with owning a dependable and easy-to-use firearm – choose the Defender 500.
